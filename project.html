Beneath The Badge
Police Shooting In USA

An Insightful Exploration of Police Shooting Incidents in the USA, I delve deep into the critical and often contentious subject of police shooting incidents in the United States. This comprehensive study leverages detailed data analysis to uncover the underlying patterns and key insights into the police shooting incidents happened in the United States from January 2015 to October 2023. I leveraged Python, along with libraries such as Pandas and NumPy for data manipulation, seaborn for visualization, and employed K-Means and Random Forest algorithms for analysis, integrating geospatial analysis to derive meaningful insights from the dataset.
